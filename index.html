Dental Surgeon | Founder – Dr. Gaur Dental Hospital

Dr. Saurabh Gaur is not only a skilled dentist but also a passionate social activist committed to uplifting his community. His efforts reflect a deep commitment to both professional excellence and social impact.

Dr. Gaurav Arya, BDS, MDS (Orthodontics)
Assistant Professor – Department of Orthodontics, NIMS University, Jaipur

Dr. Gaurav Arya is a skilled and passionate orthodontist with a strong academic and clinical background in orthodontics and dentofacial orthopedics. As a Life Member of the Indian Orthodontic Society, he combines academic rigor with a hands-on approach to patient care. Dr. Arya is known for his precision in correcting dental and jaw irregularities, offering treatments like braces, aligners, and growth modulation therapies. Currently serving as an Assistant Professor at NIMS University, Jaipur, he plays a key role in shaping future dental professionals while maintaining his commitment to patient-focused care. With a deep understanding of facial aesthetics and occlusion, Dr. Arya delivers results that enhance both function and confidence.
